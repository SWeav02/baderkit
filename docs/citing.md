# Citing

If you use BaderKit in your research please consider citing our work.

## BaderKit

The Bader class methods are described in our original software publication.

[Weaver, S. M.; Warren, S. BaderKit: A Python Package for Grid-Based Bader Charge Analysis. JOSS 2026, 11, 9943.](https://joss.theoj.org/papers/10.21105/joss.09943)

## Badelf and ElfRadii

The BadELF method and ELF radii are described in the original BadELF paper.

[Weaver, S. M.; Sundberg, J. D.; Slamowitz, C. C.; Radomsky, R. C.; Lanetti, M. G.; McRae, L. M.; Warren, S. C. Counting Electrons in Electrides. J. Am. Chem. Soc. 2023, 145, 26472–26476.](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/jacs.3c10876)

## Other

Other methods including the `BasinOverlap` class and `ElfLabeler` class are works in progress and have not been published yet. Please cite the original BaderKit paper for now.
